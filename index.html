Overview

Overview

Left In The Dark is an independant record label specializing in Dark Music.

Founded in 2015 at South Africa (Limpopo,Sekhukhune) by Evidence Maphutha widely known as Notorioux Beatz (Head of production on the record label).
Left In The Dark houses notable artists including Manila R, Kraws Bohnz, Veteran Beatz, Keezy MIK and Wasavii. As well as unknown upcoming artists...

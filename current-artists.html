1. DaNotorious Beats

Role: Excecutive Producer

Evidence Maphutha (born December 08, 1997), professionally known as Notorioux (also known as DaNotorious Beats), is South African record producer and Sound Engineer from small village in Sekhukhune, Limpopo.
He is a member of LITD and songwriter. Notorioux considered as one of the founders of Left In The Dark.

2. Veteran Beatx

2. Veteran Beatx

Role: Producer/Beats Maker

Agree Maphutha (born March 1, 2002) professionally known as Veteran Beatx. He is LITD Producer based in South Africa. He is known for his tag on his instrumentals "veteran on the beats". Some of Veteran's most notable songs include "Top 7", "Nix Mapha", and many more.
